Questions

Answered honestly.

Is seasonal data actually reliable? +
Seasonal patterns are statistical tendencies, not guarantees. Any individual year can and will deviate. The value is probabilistic: a 70% win rate over 15 years is a meaningful edge when combined with other analysis. Every TimingAX output includes explicit caveats. Use it as one tool among several.
Where does the data come from? +
Seasonal patterns are derived from historical monthly return data compiled from major financial sources and academic research. We always show observation counts and time periods so you know exactly what each figure covers. No black boxes.
Can I really cancel anytime? +
Yes. One click in account settings. No retention screens, no “tell us why” loops, no delay. You keep Pro access until the end of your billing period.
What's being built next? +

Is my data safe? +
Authentication is handled by Supabase with industry-standard encryption and row-level security. Payments are processed by Stripe with full PCI DSS compliance — we never see your card details. We do not sell your data to third parties.

No. The free tier is genuinely free forever. Three analyses per month, index funds and ETFs, Event Studies and Economic Calendar — unlimited time, no card ever required.
