FAQ

Is seasonal data reliable for trading decisions? +
Seasonal patterns are statistical tendencies from long-term data — not guarantees. They work best as one input alongside fundamentals, technicals, and macro context. Every SeasonAX analysis includes explicit caveats. Past seasonal performance does not guarantee future results.
Where does the data come from? +
Seasonal patterns are based on research-backed historical return data compiled from major market data sources. The AI analysis synthesizes documented seasonal phenomena from academic finance literature and institutional research reports.
